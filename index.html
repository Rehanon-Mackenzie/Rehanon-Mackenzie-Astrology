About Me

A few years ago I found myself at a crossroads and leaning into astrology at a much deeper level as another means of assessing who I was and what I wanted. I trained as an evolutionary astrologer. I love this take on astrology because it really give folks a base to find out about the gifts and growth areas in their chart and provides practical guidance on how to flow with life rather than against it.

Understanding your astrology for me is no different to checking the weather report before you head out for the day. Of course we can never be sure it is definitely going to rain but you will not regret packing your umbrella just in case. Knowing when the planets are passing through your own chart will give you the confidence to head out come rain or shine.

I can't wait to help you unpick your own chart and truly work out who you are and what you came here for. My approach is light-hearted and down to earth but provides a clear roadmap to your best chapter yet.

Birth Chart Reading

Prior to our session I will use your birth time, date and location to plot your birth chart so I can familiarise myself with it. Together we will then discuss your unique strengths and challenges gifted to you by the stars over a zoom call. During our time there will be ample opportunity for you to ask questions on any areas of life you are keen to focus in on.

The reading will last approximately an hour and I will then send you a recording so you can return to it for reference as there is often so much to take in initially. I guarantee you will leave feeling a renewed sense of compassion for yourself and the desire to move forward with real direction.
